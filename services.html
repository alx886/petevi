Engineering a monolithic barrier for every inch of your structure.

Attic Upgrades

Top ROI

Many homeowners overlook the attic, yet it is the primary point of energy loss. Insulating your attic is like wrapping your home in a high-performance protective shield. This monolithic layer eliminates thermal bridging, keeping your indoor temperatures stable and your family comfortable year-round. At Petevi Foam Insulation, we take meticulous care to ensure every gap is sealed, providing a massive reduction in your yearly heating and cooling costs.

  • Stops seasonal drafts
  • Extends HVAC equipment life

Basement Sealing

Comfort First

Basement insulation is the key to transforming a damp, cold lower level into a cozy and inviting living space. Our advanced spray foam technology creates a thermal break that retains heat during the harsh Ajax winters and keeps the area refreshingly cool throughout the summer. By optimizing your foundation's energy use, we eliminate the "chill" typical of Ontario basements while significantly driving down your monthly utility expenses.

  • Moisture & condensation control
  • Ready for basement finishing

Crawl Spaces

Air Quality

We are experts in navigating and insulating crawl spaces of all sizes. Proper encapsulation blocks unwanted outdoor air from seeping into your structure, which enhances your indoor air quality and eliminates the "stack effect" drafts. Our spray foam application provides a uniform barrier that safeguards your home from moisture infiltration, preventing structural rot and keeping your floors warm and dry.

  • Blocks soil gases & radon
  • Eliminates cold floor syndrome

Commercial

Durability

Industrial and commercial buildings require heavy-duty thermal solutions. We provide large-scale air sealing for warehouses, office buildings, and agricultural metal structures across the GTA. Our high-density foam creates a rigid monolithic envelope that increases structural integrity while meeting strict commercial fireproofing and building code requirements, protecting your inventory and your overhead.

  • Specialized metal building sealing
  • Fire-rated coating options
